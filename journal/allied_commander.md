## Current Strategic Assessment

*Updated Turn 1 — 09 September 1940*

Situation as at 09 September 1940. Western Desert Force is now operational with seven units concentrated in the Matruh-Alexandria corridor, hexes 2009-2016. Force composition is lean — one armoured division (7th), one infantry division (4th Indian), supporting artillery. Against this, Italian 10th Army fields an estimated nineteen units, with 63rd 'Cirene' and 62nd 'Marmarica' Divisions freshly deploying around hexes 1404-1506, roughly 600 kilometres west.

Immediate priority: do not advance prematurely. Our supply chain is sound — Cairo at 988/999, Alexandria at 250/300, forward dumps at 98 and 78 respectively — but the 7% evaporation rate is punishing. Every point spent on extended lines west is a point unavailable for the decisive period before DAK arrives at Turn 14.

Critical pressure points: three Italian units already out of supply, including 141st 'Catanzaro' and 2nd Libyan Division elements. This is exploitable. Their logistical fragility will worsen as they extend eastward. We should let them come to us, harass their supply lines with 7th Armoured's mobility, and preserve force integrity for a counter-stroke when their culminating point is reached.

The Qattara Depression secures our southern flank absolutely. Defence need only face west and northwest.

## Campaign Lessons

### Turn 1 — 09 September 1940

Confirmed: Italian supply discipline is already cracking at Turn 1. Three enemy units out of supply before a shot fired — 141st Infantry Regiment 'Catanzaro', 2nd Libyan Division HQ, and 3rd Libyan Infantry Regiment. This validates the doctrine of patient defence. Their 3% evaporation rate sounds favourable on paper, but their extended frontage from Tripoli to the wire means practical consumption outstrips capacity. We need not create their logistics crisis; it is self-inflicting.

Our own 20.3 fuel points evaporated this turn is tolerable against depot holdings of 1,238 combined at Cairo and Alexandria, plus 176 forward. But the arithmetic is sobering: at 7% compounding, forward dump Beta at hex 1908 (78 points) loses roughly 5.5 points per turn doing nothing. Every fuel dump positioned west of Alexandria must justify its existence operationally.

Key lesson: husband forward fuel. Concentrate 7th Armoured at hex 2010 near Dump Alpha. Do not disperse. Wait for the Italian culmination.
